Discover Girona

Where History, Cuisine, and Nature Converge

Historical Charm

Discover Girona's medieval old town, complete with narrow cobblestone streets, the iconic Girona Cathedral, and the well-preserved Jewish Quarter, offering a glimpse into the city's multicultural past.

Culinary Delights

Indulge in Girona's renowned culinary scene, including Michelin-starred restaurants like El Celler de Can Roca, vibrant markets such as Mercat del Lleó, and traditional tapas bars serving authentic Catalan flavors.

Natural Beauty

Surround yourself with stunning natural landscapes, from the rugged coastline of Costa Brava to nearby natural parks like Parc Natural dels Aiguamolls de l'Empordà, offering opportunities for hiking, cycling, and water sports.

Your tour guide

Genís Muner

Embark on an unforgettable journey through Girona's enchanting Old Town with Genís, where medieval streets, ancient landmarks, and captivating stories intertwine, offering a unique and immersive exploration of this historic gem.
